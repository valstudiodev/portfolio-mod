Your Website
in 5 steps

The process ensures that I create a website tailored to your business needs.

Contact

You reach out with your idea or project.

  • We get to know each other better.
  • Send a short description or message about your idea.
  • Understand the goals you have for your website.

Discussion

We talk about goals, design vision, and technical details.

  • Define project structure and main features.
  • Discuss visual style, mood, and color palette
  • Agree on deadlines and workflow format.

Development

I build and optimize your website for performance and usability.

  • Turn design into a responsive and functional site.
  • Add animations, effects, and interactive elements.
  • Test on different devices and browsers.

Launch

You receive a ready, responsive, and well-structured website.

  • Final review and optimization for SEO & speed.
  • Upload files to hosting or help with deployment.
  • Make sure everything works perfectly.

Support

I stay in touch to make sure everything runs smoothly.

  • Monitor performance and fix possible bugs.
  • Apply updates or small improvements.
  • Stay available for future collaboration.
